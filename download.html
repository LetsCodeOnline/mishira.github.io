Step 2: Install Mishira

Launch the Mishira installer that you just downloaded and follow the on-screen instructions to install Mishira to your computer. The default installation settings are recommended for most users.

Step 3: Launch Mishira

Once the installer has completed it will immediately launch Mishira if you have the setting selected. You can launch Mishira again at any time by double-clicking the icon on your desktop or by selecting Mishira from the Windows start menu.
