Welcome to Mishira, the next level of video game live streaming.

Mishira is a personal audiovisual production tool used for editing and broadcasting live video over the internet that has been specifically designed for people who play video games. With an aim to increase the level of professionalism in video game live streams Mishira focuses on making professional video editing features more accessible and easier to use by the average gamer while they are actually playing a video game themselves. Mishira is completely free and open source software.
Mishira allows you to capture, record and broadcast your PC's desktop and speakers and mix them together with your own images, text, webcams, microphones, visual effects and transitions to form a single professional video broadcast. Not only that but Mishira also does everything using the least amount of system resources possible in order to not interfere with any videos games or other software that are also running on the same system.

Broadcast and record live video

Broadcast live video to websites such as Twitch, YouTube, Justin.tv and Ustream while recording a higher quality version to your hard drive for later editing. You can even broadcast to multiple websites or record to multiple files at the same time with different video settings! 

Powerful built-in video editor

Edit your videos while you're recording them with the powerful built-in editor with live video preview. Add images, text, webcams, visual effects and transitions in real time without the need for any external software.

Hardware accelerated video game capture

With hardware acceleration you can capture graphics intensive video games and other software at their full framerate for those extra smooth recordings.

Lightweight construction

Mishira has been designed from the ground up to use the least amount of system resources possible so that you can get maximum performance out of your computer while you are broadcasting.

Easy to use

Not a professional video encoding engineer? No problem! Mishira can automatically figure out all the highly technical details of video encoding for you so you can focus entirely on the content of your broadcasts. No more trying to work out what the best resolution, bitrate and "preset" your internet connection and computer can support through trial-and-error!

Compatible with a large variety of video games

Mishira's hardware accelerated video game capture has been tested with a large variety of video games ranging from very old DirectX 9 and OpenGL 1 games to the latest and greatest DirectX 11 and OpenGL 4 ones. Have some very obscure video game you want to broadcast? There's a high chance that it'll work.

Professional level audio

Mishira's advanced built-in audio mixer gives you more power over what your viewers hear. You can add an unlimited amount of audio inputs and mix them together with individial volume controls so they are never too quiet or too loud. Each audio input has its own separate professional VU and peak meters so you can quickly see at a glance how loud your audio is without having to hear it yourself.

Low network impact

If you want to live stream online multiplayer video games then you'll love Mishira's network subsystem. It has been designed and specifically tuned to cause the least amount of network lag as possible even if you're using most of your internet connection for broadcasting video. Best of all it's completely automatic and requires no configuration!

Interact with your viewers

If you're broadcasting video games make it even more entertaining for your viewers by adding a webcam and microphone so they can see who's actually behind the controls. By being able to see your facial expressions as you experience the game your viewers will feel more immersed in the game world itself as they'll feel like they are right there next to you.
